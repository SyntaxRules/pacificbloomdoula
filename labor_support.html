Labor Support

Labor is a journey. Like any adventure it can take time and preparation to make sure you have all the tools you need to get to the end. What are your goals in this journey? A doula can provide resources and education so you can make the best decisions for your birthing plan. Establishing a relationship with your doula means we’ve got your back physically and emotionally whether it’s in a hospital, birthing center or at home. Support doesn’t stop there. Doulas also support the birthing person's partner so they have the confidence and knowledge they need during labor and delivery.

  • Free initial consultation
  • 1-2 prenatal visits to go over your birth plan, coping techniques, education and questions
  • Unlimited call, text and email support
  • 24/7 on-call availability at 36 weeks. (Arrangements made for high-risk and twins)
  • Guidance and education for supporting partner
  • Up to 2 hours of immediate postpartum support to help settle your family
  • 1st latch/bottle feeding assistance
  • 1 postpartum followup within the week of birth
  • 4 weeks of postpartum email, text and calling
